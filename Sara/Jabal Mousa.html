Jabal Mousa
Details:
Jabal Mousa exist in Suoth Sinai,Egypt Its height is 2285 over the sea ,It is the second height after Sant Kathreen Mountain and it has a high religious meaning for every religion because it is believed that the mountain is the place where Mousa received the Ten Commandments from Allah . Jabal Mousa is known for the very beautiful sight that you will when you are on the peak of the mountain and people go there to see the sunrise and sunset ,After watching the you will be amazed from the silence at sunset and the sound at sunrise. You must go to see this gourney.

1-No Children
2-Must Have Heavy Clothes
3-Food And Water For At Least 5 Hours.
