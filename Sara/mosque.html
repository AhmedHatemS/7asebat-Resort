AL-MUSTAFA MOSQUE
DETAILS:
AL-MUSTAFA MOSQUE IS ONE OF THE MOST FAMOUS MOSQUES IN SHARM EL-SHEIKH. IT OPENED ON OCTOBER 26, 2007 TO BECOME ONE OF THE CITY'S TOURIST ATTRACTIONS AT A COST OF EGP 17 MILLION RAISED THROUGH DONATIONS TO THE ASSOCIATION OF THE LOVERS OF MUSTAFA IN SHARM EL-SHEIKH IN ADDITION TO GOVERNMENT FUNDING. THE MOSQUE HAS ANAREA OF 27,000 METERS AND A HEIGHT OF 12 METERS AND THE HEIGHT OF ITS MINARETS 71 METERS AND ITS DOME HEIGHT 31 METERS AND ITS GARDEN INCLUDES 143 PALM TREES, 85 FRUIT TREES AND 10 ORNAMENTAL PALMS, AND THE MOSQUE HAS 30 CONCRETE LIGHTING POLES AND 49 IRON LIGHTING POLES, AND CONTAINS 31TOILETS, AND ACCOMMODATES INSIDE ABOUT 3000 WORSHIPPERS AND 1000 WORSHIPPERS IN THE AREA OF THE DOME ATTACHED TO IT
