ROOMS

Make our Red Sea stay unforgettable by staying in one of our comfortable rooms/suites and villas at 7asebat Resort Beach. Contemporary designs and natural daylight complement high quality Egyptian cotton fabrics and elegant furniture. All our 5-star rooms feature tea and coffee making facilities, air conditioning, LCD TV, hairdryer, minibar and free WiFi.

Choose a Deluxe Room with your choice of city, pool or sea views. Alternatively, option for more space in a Deluxe Suite or spoil yourself with a stay in our Pool Villa Lagoon. Our spacious Family Rooms can accommodate up to three children and offer a balcony with views of either the mountains or Red Sea.

If you enjoy keeping active, take a dip in our beautiful swimming pool, or visit the luxury 24 -hour health club, which has a state-of-the-art gym and steam rooms.

All our rooms include:

  • Free WiFi Internet access
  • Complimentary tea and coffee making facilities
  • LCD TV
  • Minibar
  • Egyptian cotton fabrics
